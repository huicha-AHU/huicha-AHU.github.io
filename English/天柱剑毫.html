Tianzhu Jian Hao Tea

Tianzhu Jian Hao is a kind of green tea. The name comes from three parts. Tianzhu Mountain, the origin of place of the tea, is a famous mountain where the tea garden is shrouded by fogs all the year round in Anhui province, while Jian means sword, coming from the shape of the dry tea which looks like as flat as a sword. Hao is the white down of tea leaves, implying the tenderness and freshness. Every year before and after Grain Rain, farmers begin to pick new tea. Only the bud and first two leaves are chosen so that the yield is very limited. As a result, the tea is extremely precious. Tianzhu Jian Hao, with its excellent quality, unique style and appearance has been evaluated as one of the national famous teas in 1985 National Famous Tea Exhibition.

Tianzhu Jian Hao produces in the area around Qianshan County, Anhui Province. The tea gardens usually settle in the mountainous areas 500 meters above sea level. Because the environment is misty which are suitable for tea growth, the tea boast a natural faint fragrance.

The shape of the tea leaves is flat and straight, shaped like a sword. The leaves are bright green, covered with white downs. Its floral aroma is elegant and lasting.

Pour 80℃ water into the cup to fill one third of the cup, then throw the dry tea in and tilt the cup slowly to ensure the leaves to be immersed by water. After that pour the water from a relatively high position to fill the whole cup. With the flush, the leaves will dance with the water and the tea fragrance begins to diffuse. Famous for its “green leaves, clear soup, mellow flavor”, Tianzhu Jian Hao tastes rich at first and gives a feeling of freshness when washed down. And its fragrance and sweetness will linger for a long time in mouth.

Back to Tang dynasty, the Classic of Tea written by sage Lu Yǜ has recorded that in Qianshan (where Tianzhu Mountain is located) people produced tea. Coming to the North Song Dynasty, tea produced in Tianzhu Mountain was highly recommended. However, the tea, without any reason, suddenly disappeared and the techniques of Tianzhu Jian Hao were almost on the verge of extinction. In order to explore Tianzhu Mountain tea resources and adapt to the development of tourism, after six years of research and trail production, the famous tea “Tianzhu Jian Hao” was successfully created in 1985, which makes Tianzhu famous tea shine again.

Hui Tea “Going Out”

As early as in Ming and Qing Dynasties, Huizhou Tea was famous with the power of Huizhou merchants. However, it is difficult now for Huizhou Tea to go overseas. One of the difficulties is the cultural obstacle. The West knows litter of Hui Tea. The international status of Hui Tea needs to be improved. Under the background of “ going out” national strategy, the promotion of Hui Tea culture can lay a foundation for internationalization of Hui Tea. Hui tea has a long history and rich connotations. Introducing Hui Tea culture, as the first step of popularization, can help Anhui tea enterprises to “ go out” and bring new opportunities for the economic development of Anhui.
