Yuexi Cuilan Tea

Yuexi Cuilan Tea is a new famous tea, produced in Yuexi county, Anhui province, where is the abdomen of Dabie Mountains. It is named as Cuilan (green orchid) because it is alike the shape of orchid flower.

Central part of Dabie Mountain, Yuexi county, Anqing City, Anhui Province.。

The tea leaves are slender, green color, shaped like orchids. The floral aroma is high and long-lasting. The tea soup is light green and bright. The taste is mellow and fresh. The tea leaves a sweetish aftertaste.

The shape of the tea is connected by buds, stretched into a flower, shaped like orchids.

The main characteristic of its fragrance is “light and bright”, which bring a feeling of freshness.

The leaves remain green and flower-like even after brewing.

Yuexi Cuilan was created in 1983. In 1996, it won the gold medal of the Agricultural Fair, the title of “Top Ten Famous Teas in Anhui Province”, and the Gold Prize of the China Tea Cup. In 2005, it was recognized as “the best-selling brand in Anhui Province” at the first tea fair in China (Anhui); in 2006, it was recognized as “Anhui Famous Brand Agricultural Products” by the People's Government of Anhui Province. From 2010 to 2012, it became the designated tea for catering national guests, and the appointed tea supplying in two sessions in 2011. In April 2013, he won the Gold Award in the third China Tea Expo in Beijing.

Hui Tea “Going Out”

As early as in Ming and Qing Dynasties, Huizhou Tea was famous with the power of Huizhou merchants. However, it is difficult now for Huizhou Tea to go overseas. One of the difficulties is the cultural obstacle. The West knows litter of Hui Tea. The international status of Hui Tea needs to be improved. Under the background of “ going out” national strategy, the promotion of Hui Tea culture can lay a foundation for internationalization of Hui Tea. Hui tea has a long history and rich connotations. Introducing Hui Tea culture, as the first step of popularization, can help Anhui tea enterprises to “ go out” and bring new opportunities for the economic development of Anhui.
